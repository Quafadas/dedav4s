    • Dedav4s

    Declarative data visualization for scala - a scala plotting concept.

    It is written exclusively in scala 3 (currently), but will work with scala 2.13.6+ via forward compatibility.

    Elevator Pitch

    If that worked, then you're ready to go! See the plot targets to understand what happened, and the examples for suggestions on how to use and extend the concepts.

    Background Information

    This is a thin shim around vega and vega lite. It is aimed at repl, interactive environments and exploratory analysis

    It pays to have an understanding (or at least some idea of what those are), both Vega & Vega-Lite. It may be worth taking a few minutes to orient yourself with this talk/demo from the creators at the Interactive Data Lab (IDL) at University of Washington.

    If you are interested in plotting in general, I think that you will not regret learning a declarative paradigm.
